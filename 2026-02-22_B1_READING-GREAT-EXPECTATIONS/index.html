BIO

CHARLES DICKENS | Mirror of Society

Career: English novelist and journalist. Known for 15 major novels.
Background: Had to work in a factory at age 12 because his family had money problems and his father went to prison for debt.
Legacy: Famous for memorable characters and showing the hard lives of working people.

TASK 1 CHECK | Experience

Hard lives of working people
[About the Author] 'This difficult experience influenced his novels, which often show the hard lives of working British people...'

Dickens used his own pain to tell the stories of those who couldn't speak for themselves.

TASK 1 CHECK | Fame

Memorable characters
[About the Author] 'Dickens’ memorable characters have made his novels famous...'

Characters like Pip and Magwitch are so vivid they feel real to readers even today.
Discuss the answer with the class.

TASK 1 CHECK | Theme

Development from a boy to a man
[About the Author] 'The story shows the development of the main character from a boy to a man.'

It is a 'Bildungsroman'—a story of growing up and finding one's place in the world.
Discuss the answer with the class.

TASK 6 CHECK | Lonely Child

AGREE (A)
[Para 1] 'My parents had died... I used to visit the churchyard... to look at their names.'

Visiting graves alone as a child suggests a deep sense of isolation.
Discuss the answer with the class.

TASK 6 CHECK | Happy Home

DISAGREE (D)
[Para 9] 'She clearly felt that Joe and I caused her trouble, and she frequently complained about it.'

Pip's sister is very harsh and makes him feel like a burden.
Discuss the answer with the class.

TASK 6 CHECK | The Young Man

AGREE (A)
[Para 17] 'He was dressed in grey, too, and had an iron chain on his leg.'

The matching chains and grey clothes prove he is also an escapee.
Discuss the answer with the class.

TASK 6 CHECK | Truly Bad?

DISAGREE (D)
[Para 19] 'Thank you, my boy. You've been good to a poor man.'

He shows gratitude to Pip, hinting at a hidden spark of humanity.
Discuss the answer with the class.

TASK 6 CHECK | Enemies

AGREE (A)
[Para 21] 'I'll find him, and I'll finish with him! That villain!'

The older man is furious and wants to 'finish' the younger one.
Discuss the answer with the class.
