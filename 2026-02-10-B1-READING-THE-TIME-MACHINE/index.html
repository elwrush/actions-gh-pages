STRATEGY | Scanning for Facts

⌘WHAT: Find specific dates and names quickly.
⚡WHY: To answer questions without reading every single word.

STRATEGY | True, False, or Not Mentioned?

TRUE: The text says exactly this (or uses synonyms).
FALSE: The text says the opposite or something different.
NOT MENTIONED: The text doesn't say anything about this at all.
