# Raw Content - Sports Interviews (B2)

## Exercise 5: Profiles
**Petra (14)**: From Czech Republic, now in US tennis school. Trains 5 hours/day + 3 hours school. Pressure to succeed.
**Holly (16)**: Soccer player, top scorer. Used to be shy, now confident. Wants to be a coach. Parents proud.
**Newman (18)**: Wheelchair basketball. Accident 3 years ago -> depressed. Joined club last year. Wants to play in Paralympics.

## Exercise 6: Quotes & Reported Speech
**Reporting Verbs**: admit, explain, complain, offer, deny, insist

1. "I never have any free time. I'm always playing sports or studying." (Petra - *complain/explain*)
2. "That's wrong, I didn't give up on sports. I was waiting for another opportunity." (Newman? - *deny*)
3. "Do you like soccer? I'll get you some tickets to my next game." (Holly - *offer*)
4. "You must let me play in this game, coach. I need the practice so I can compete in the Paralympics one day!" (Newman - *insist*)
5. "I often feel anxious before a game. I worry about not being good enough." (Petra - *admit*)
6. "My mom didn't like it at first, but she never misses a game now!" (Holly - *explain/admit*)
